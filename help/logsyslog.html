Write Non-Access Log Entries Into Syslog

Write non-access messages into the syslog facility. (This setting does not affect access messages, which will be written to the DansGuardian log file regardless.)

The actual destination of such messages is determined by your Syslog configuration. Messages produced by DansGuardian have a facility of USER. Most of the messages produced by DansGuardian have a level of ERR. Some have a level of INFO. Although other levels are not currently used, they are possible in the future.
