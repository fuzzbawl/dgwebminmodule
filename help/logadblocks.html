Log Advertisement Blocks

To some extent, DansGuardian can filter Advertisements while it's filtering everything else. Anything in a list tagged with #listcategory "ADs" (note "AD" must be UPPERcase) will be given special treatment. You may find this setting useful to keep your logs smaller and more relevant by omitting all the blocked advertisement accesses. Write non-access messages into the syslog facility.
